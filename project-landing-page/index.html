PaperTrail is an advanced yet user-friendly text editor designed to streamline your editing experience. Built with Java, Maven, and the FlatLaf IJ theme, it offers a modern and intuitive interface with a comprehensive set of features to boost productivity.

Features

  • Clean Interface: Enjoy a clutter-free workspace that minimizes distractions and maximizes focus on your content.
  • Multiple Tabs: Seamlessly switch between multiple documents within the same window for efficient multitasking.
  • Find and Replace: Quickly locate specific words or phrases and replace them with ease using the find and replace functionality.
  • Insert Date/Time: Instantly insert the current date and time into your document with a simple keystroke, saving you time and effort.
  • Undo/Redo: Easily correct mistakes and revert changes with the undo and redo functionality, ensuring a stress-free editing experience.
  • Font Settings: Customize the appearance of your text by adjusting the font, size, and style to suit your preferences and enhance readability.
  • Theme Settings: Personalize your editing environment with a choice of light and dark themes, providing optimal comfort for extended editing sessions.
  • Zoom In/Out: Adjust the zoom level to magnify or reduce the size of your text, accommodating different screen sizes and visual preferences.
  • Status Bar: Stay informed about your document's status with a convenient status bar that displays helpful information such as word count, line number, and cursor position.
  • Open With: Each file extension (.inf, .ini, .log, .ps1, .psd1, .psm1, .scp, .txt, .wtx) now has its own association key and registry entries, enabling seamless integration with your preferred applications.
  • Improved File Handling: Experience enhanced flexibility and convenience in managing various file types, ensuring smooth and efficient workflow.

Installation

  1. Download the latest release from the GitHub releases page.
  2. Run the installer and follow the prompts to complete the installation.

Usage

After installing PaperTrail, you can open it by clicking on the PaperTrail icon in your applications menu. To create a new document, click on the "New" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + N. To open an existing document, click on the "Open" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + O.

You can customize the editor settings by clicking on the "Settings" button in the toolbar. Here, you can change the font, size, and style of the text, as well as choose between a light and dark theme.

To find and replace text in the document, click on the "Find" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + F. To insert the current date and time into the document, click on the "Insert Date/Time" button in the toolbar or press F5.

To save the document, click on the "Save" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + S. To save the document under a new name, click on the "Save As" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + Shift + S.

To print the document, click on the "Print" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + P.

To close the current tab, click on the "Close Tab" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + W. To close the entire application, click on the "Close Window" button in the toolbar or press Ctrl + Shift + W.
